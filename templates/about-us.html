A Warm Welcome to

NRI Wellbeing Services

NRI Wellbeing Services is a thought of Non Resident Indian – NRI who came back to India after staying abroad for more than a Decade and drove across so many uncomfortable situations to get done his requirements in India. He landed back in India with an aim and great intention to relieve the NRIs from their drop back by serving all levels and make it hassle free for the NRI to Complete his works in India.

This platform is especially designed to cater the whole needs of the Non Resident Indians. "A quite friendly organization as a one-point solution for all NRI needs in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh States, INDIA"

We, at NRI Wellbeing along with our most professional team, extend our whole range of essential services to all NRI’s who are indeed need of them.

While we undertake your job in India you can be sit back and relax comfortably. We know exactly what the NRI is looking for in terms of service, quality and trust. There are times when a particular task maybe urgent / important and NRI look for assistance. We deem it our responsibility to give you "Utmost Peace of Mind" by taking care of your entire tasks here in INDIA while you are abroad.

We are on this mission to make the Life of NRI easier by providing our professional services for all day to day issues and other routine tasks in India. To make use of your actual holidays in INDIA with optimal time utilisation by not wasting your valuable time in running around to get all the things done such as logistics, travel assistance, documentation etc. going forward, all the needs of NRI, from a pin to plane can be organised in a most friendly way and make your holidays stress free as a meaningful & valuable vacation.

Our healthcare services team will look after the health of all your family members while you are away, since it might not always be possible for you, to be present in person with them. Here after, you no longer need to worry about your family how they will cope-up with the situations. We make sure that, they get the best of emergency care and medical assistance on time. You can have that relief by knowing someone is responsibly handling everything on your behalf in the way you want.

We learnt that, you may worry about your family waiting in Government Offices or dealing with other officials to get your documents. Here on your behalf, for everything we will put present ourselves in your shoes to serve you by providing our best support to your family from running pillar to post in order to get the documents they need. Our technical team will handle everything from Birth certificate to Ration cards, Aadhar cards, Voter id, Pan Cards, Educational certificates on the way covering all such documentation and activities with ease. Apart from providing concierge services we also provide other services, like property development, property management, Assets management, Health care, Taxation, Legal, Insurance, Banking certificates, attestations, educational related certificates, Tours and Travels etc.

One of the key requirements for most overseas Indians is property management. In such times When NRI do not want to let out their property and kept it lock and idle for long time, whenever they visit in India and try to using it. Where their valuable time will be vanished in cleaning, mobilizing and arranging the house hold things etc. We at NRI wellbeing services can assist you in getting these petty things done in advance even before you reach India. Also we organise your trip in India as per your pre-determined schedule including accommodation, transport and telecom services etc. everywhere. NRI Wellbeing will take the opportunity to organise your itinerary at your best convenience and save your precious time.

Our operations are very simple and friendly. Once we get an enquiry we will respond within 24 hour and get the task details & nativity details both in India and as well abroad. Based on the task assessment the approximate estimate will be prepared and submitted to you for approval. In parallel to that, the origin verification will be done based on the details provided by you for security purposes. Once we get an approval for the estimate and the Origin verification report says it is genuine, you will be directed to the payment gateway. Once payment is over, we will proceed to Open a Job Card along with other formal documentation and with a unique Membership ID. Then the job process starts. After your order is confirmed with us, we will provide a login id & password, then you can login to your My Account and check the status of your orders, also you will receive regular updates from us to your registered email. Pricing for the job will be actuals + Our service charges + GST. Which means real value for your money.
